# Profile Photos

Place your profile photos here:

- `sattvik.jpg` — Photo of Sattvik (shown on his profile card in the "Who am I?" selector)
- `riya.jpg` — Photo of Riya (shown on her profile card in the "Who am I?" selector)

## Recommended specs
- Square crop works best (1:1 ratio)
- At least 200×200px
- JPG or PNG both work — just name them exactly `sattvik.jpg` and `riya.jpg`

Until you add the photos, styled avatar initials (S / R) will show instead.
